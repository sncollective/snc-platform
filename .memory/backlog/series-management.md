# Series Management

Container concept for albums, podcast shows, video series, and written series (zines, books). Separate management pages at `/manage/series/$seriesId`. Common shape across series types: title, description, cover image, and ordering strategy — with type-specific defaults. Individual singles exist independently or belong to a series. Needs full scoping before implementation.
