# Connected Accounts for Simulcast (OAuth Platform Integrations)

OAuth2 flows for Twitch, YouTube, and similar platforms to auto-provision RTMP simulcast destinations from connected accounts. Unlocks metadata sync (stream title, category), platform analytics, and go-live notifications pushed to connected platforms. Builds on the existing RTMP destination management foundation. Part of Phase 8: Simulcast + Dashboard.
