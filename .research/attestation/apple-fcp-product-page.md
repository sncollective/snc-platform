# Attestation: Final Cut Pro Product Page (Apple)

## Paraphrased summary

Final Cut Pro is available as a one-time purchase at $299.99 (Mac) or as part of Apple Creator Studio subscription at $12.99/month or $129/year (which bundles Logic Pro, Motion, Compressor, Pixelmator Pro). Platform support is Mac-only (requires macOS 15.6 or later, 8GB RAM minimum); iPad support exists via a separate app (requires Apple M1 chip or later). No Windows or Linux support.

The product page highlights beat detection for music synchronization and advanced audio editing as relevant capabilities for music video production. Live Multicam support for up to 4 devices is mentioned.

The page does NOT document: scripting/automation capabilities, EDL/AAF/FCP XML interchange format specifics, network media source support, or headless/background rendering.

iMovie import is mentioned as a capability.
