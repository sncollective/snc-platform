# Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)

## Paraphrased Summary

The CC BY 4.0 license deed defines the canonical form of attribution for licensed creative works. While S/NC streams are not necessarily CC-licensed, this document establishes the compositional logic for what full attribution of a derivative work requires.

## What Is Permitted

- **Sharing:** "Copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format for any purpose, even commercially"
- **Adapting:** "Remix, transform, and build upon the material for any purpose, even commercially"

Both permissions are irrevocable as long as license terms are followed.

## Attribution Requirements

Proper attribution must include:
- Creator name and attribution parties (if supplied)
- Copyright notice
- License notice
- Disclaimer notice
- Link to the material
- **Indication that modifications were made** (in version 4.0)

Credit may be provided "in any reasonable manner, but not in any way that suggests the licensor endorses you or your use."

## Platform-Relevant Restriction

"You may not apply legal terms or technological measures that legally restrict others from doing anything the license permits." Platforms cannot impose additional restrictions beyond what the license allows.

## Automatic Termination

Rights terminate automatically on license violation. Under CC 4.0, a 30-day cure period applies before rights are lost permanently.

## Key Passages

> Attribution must include "an indication that modifications were made" in version 4.0.

> "In any reasonable manner, but not in any way that suggests the licensor endorses you or your use."

## Structural Notes

The deed itself notes it "has no legal value" — the full legal code governs. The deed is a human-readable summary of the license terms.
