# Laylo — creator fan-capture-and-conversion platform (landing page)

## Paraphrased summary

Laylo is a purpose-built direct-to-fan platform: artists capture fan contact opt-ins (email, SMS, Instagram DM) across many surfaces, into a list the artist owns, then auto-message that list when a "drop" (release / ticket / merch) goes live. It is the category-defining example of the buy-a-vendor end of the capture→convert space for musicians.

## Key passages (page-stated)

- Captures "email, SMS, and DM opt-ins" into audience lists the user "truly own[s]."
- Capture methods: **RSVP pages** ("1-click" signup), **Instagram** stories/posts/reels, **keyword triggers** (reply-with-keyword), automated **Instagram DM** capture.
- Integrations: **Shopify** (purchase + checkout opt-in sync), **Spotify** (presave), Google Tag Manager, **FEVO** (ticket-purchase sync), Instagram DMs.
- Conversion route: "automatically message[s] your list when your drop goes live," directing fans to tickets / merch / content.
- Page-stated stats (vendor marketing figures): "10,000+ creators", "$1B in tickets, merch, & content drops", "150M+ Fan interactions", drops convert "7x better" with their landing pages.

## Structural metadata

Vendor landing page (marketing surface) — stats are self-reported and uncorroborated. **No Stripe / subscription / patronage integration is stated** — commerce hooks are Shopify (merch) + FEVO (tickets), not recurring-subscription. Relevant gap for a platform whose commerce domain is Stripe + patronage.

## Disconfirming note

The stats are vendor-marketing claims with no independent source; treat as illustrative of the category, not as measured fact. The absence of a Stripe/subscription hook means Laylo does not cover the platform's specific donate/subscribe conversion target out of the box.
