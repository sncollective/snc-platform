# Hosted EPK / press page for label artists

A hosted electronic press kit page: press photos, bio, streaming and video
links, press coverage highlights, and a downloadable asset bundle — the page
a publicist or journalist lands on from a pitch email.

Motivating context (S/NC Records, Animal Future campaign): the campaign runs
DIY press outreach through fall 2026 and a publicist-led push January–March
2027. It ships a lightweight shareable kit (PDF + shared folder) immediately
and does not wait on this page — but a hosted page is the better long-term
surface, and the need generalizes to any future label artist. Likely
adjacencies on the platform side: creator profile surfaces, the
media/imgproxy stack for press photo delivery.

No campaign deadline binds this item; it lands whenever this queue
prioritizes it, and the campaign's kit folds into it on arrival.
