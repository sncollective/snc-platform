# Open Questions

Unresolved design questions that need answers before or during scoping — content gating granularity across protocols, federated comment moderation ownership, and inbound federation policy (open vs. allowlist/blocklist).

**Bullets to split / scope out when this is promoted:**

- Content gating interaction with protocols — granularity of creator opt-in (per-piece vs per-tier vs per-protocol)
- Federated comment moderation — creator responsibility, platform responsibility, or shared
- Inbound federation scope — open federation vs allowlist/blocklist for instances/relays
