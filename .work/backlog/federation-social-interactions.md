# Federated Social Interactions

Cross-protocol social layer — unified reactions, threaded comments, Nostr payments, cross-protocol follows, @-mention resolution, and moderation propagation across ActivityPub, AT Protocol, Nostr, and Polycentric.

**Bullets to split / scope out when this is promoted:**

- Cross-protocol reactions — AP Like, AT like record, Nostr NIP-25 reaction, Polycentric vouch
- Federated comments — replies from all four protocols threaded alongside native comments
- Nostr payments — NIP-57 zaps (Lightning tips), NIP-47 Wallet Connect
- Cross-protocol follow/subscribe — single S/NC follow propagates to creator's enabled protocols
- Cross-protocol mentions — @-mention resolution across AP, AT, Nostr, and Polycentric
- Moderation propagation — blocks and mutes applied on S/NC propagate to federated interactions
