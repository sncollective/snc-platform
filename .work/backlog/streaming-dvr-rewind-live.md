# DVR/Rewind Live

YouTube-style live rewind — viewers can pause and rewind a live stream by up to a configurable window (e.g., 30 minutes). Enabled by SRS native DVR maintaining a rolling buffer. Vidstack player needs a DVR-aware seek bar that distinguishes between live edge and rewound positions. Depends on SRS DVR recording being configured.
